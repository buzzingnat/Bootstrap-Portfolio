About Me

I love building websites with CSS3, HTML5, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, and WordPress.

For fun, I play card, computer, and table-top rpg games. I sketch, paint, knit, write, and occasionally sing. My toddler-wrangling skills are top-notch. My pizza-creating game is on point. In my life, there is never a bad time to drink some (decaf) coffee. In 2017 I discovered the writing of Brandon Sanderson, and 16 books later I can still say I like his writing. I love books about either spaceships or dragons, and several of my favorite books involve both at the same time.

I got my first taste of web design from the CSS Zen Garden in 2004, and I have been making online sites and web apps as a hobby ever since.
